Our Story

Founded on autonomy and intention

AIRIK ART & ENTERTAINMENT CO. (AAEC) was founded in March 2022 by multidisciplinary artist and creative director Airik Crawford. After years of working as a freelance filmmaker, photographer, and visual artist, Airik sought something greater than simply producing content: ownership over the stories being told, and a creative practice rooted in art direction, elegance, and intentionality.

AAEC was created as a response to an industry that often prioritizes speed over substance and trends over timelessness. Here, visual experiences are treated as works of art, and stories are preserved with care, beauty, and purpose.

The Founder

Airik Crawford

Eric Boyd Crawford Jr., professionally known as Airik Crawford, was raised on the West Side of Chicago and embodies the spirit of the city itself: resilient, imaginative, and unapologetically bold. A graduate of Knox College with a Bachelor of Arts in Fine Arts, he approaches visual storytelling from the perspective of an artist first, drawing on painting, design, composition, and creative theory. His work draws inspiration from fine art, architecture, fashion, cinema, music, community, Black culture, and history.

Mission

To create meaningful visual experiences that preserve stories, inspire culture, and elevate communities through creativity and excellence.

What We Believe

Stories deserve beauty. Culture deserves preservation. Art deserves discipline. Creativity deserves professionalism. Excellence is an act of service. Legacy matters.

What We Are Not

AAEC is not content creators. AAEC is not trend chasers. AAEC is not volume-driven. AAEC does not pursue virality at the expense of meaning.
