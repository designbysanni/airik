The Guest House Chicago: It's A Marathon

Documenting a nonprofit's runners, supporters, and partners at the Bank of America Chicago Marathon — and the mission behind them.

It's A Marathon — Film

The Brief

The Guest House Chicago provides complimentary lodging for patients and families traveling to Chicago for life-saving medical treatment. During the marathon, AAEC documented the organization's team while capturing the stories behind their mission.

The Approach

We managed the production from on-site coverage through post — coordinating logistics across multiple marathon locations and conducting interviews with participants, stakeholders, and sponsors — delivering a film that raises awareness for the families the organization serves.
