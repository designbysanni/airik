The Devine Feminine

A fashion and lifestyle campaign for Kinky Behavior that explores themes of femininity, spirituality, and self-expression through cinematic storytelling.

Watch

The Devine Feminine — Commercial

The Brief

Kinky Behavior is more than a fashion label — from handcrafted handbags and jewelry to original wardrobe styling, every piece embodies confidence, individuality, and an earthy, elevated aesthetic. The Devine Feminine was created to communicate that philosophy visually and invite viewers into the world built around the brand.

The Approach

Across multiple productions filmed in Illinois and Atlanta, AAEC developed cinematic concepts emphasizing natural environments, rich textures, dreamlike imagery, and intentional pacing — every decision, from location and camera movement to color grade and edit, designed to keep the fashion, jewelry, and styling as the focal point.
