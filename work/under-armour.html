The Brief

Working through Intersport, AAEC traveled the southeastern United States documenting elite athletes as they received their official Under Armour All-America jerseys, competed in regional camps, and prepared for the national showcase — content built to connect fans with the next generation of football talent.

The Approach

We produced documentary-style features on top recruits including Owen Pappoe and Noah Cain, alongside behind-the-scenes coverage with athletes and their families, feeding Instagram and Snapchat content that helped grow the Under Armour Football community to more than 100,000 followers.
