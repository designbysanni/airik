A Greater Good Foundation: Let's Grow Fest

A community wellness festival in Maywood, Illinois bringing together more than 1,000 attendees for a day centered on health, education, fitness, and connection.

Let's Grow Fest — Official Recap

The Brief

Held at Proviso East High School, the festival featured local leaders, youth organizations, wellness instructors, vendors, artists, performers, and elected officials — an engaging experience celebrating the power of community.

The Approach

As lead media coordinator, AAEC directed the event's visual storytelling from production through post — overseeing on-site coverage, camera operation, and content direction, then editing the official recap and social content that extended the event's impact beyond the day itself.
