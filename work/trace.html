Chicago Park District: TRACE

Telling the story of Teens Reimagining Art, Community, and Environment — a Chicago Park District program that has empowered thousands of young people for more than two decades.

Watch

TRACE — Program Film

The Brief

The project was created to tell the program's story while inspiring future participants, stakeholders, donors, and community partners to invest in its continued success.

The Approach

AAEC directed, shot, and edited the documentary-style film — and went further, working directly with youth as teaching artists: teaching camera operation, video editing, and commercial production while helping students develop creative and technical skills.
