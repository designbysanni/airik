Spirits Campaign

Brand and lifestyle photography for a spirits campaign that reads as premium without tipping into cliché.

The Brief

Campaign imagery for a spirits brand that needed to read as premium without the standard bottle-on-a-table product-shot convention — the bottle had to earn its place in a real scene, not just sit in front of one.

The Approach

We shot the product in context — real settings, real moments, controlled lighting built around the glass and the pour — so the campaign feels like a lifestyle story first and an advertisement second. Color and composition were kept consistent across the set so the images work individually or as a single campaign.
