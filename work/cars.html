Dodge — Up For The Challenge

The Brief

Centered on the theme “Up for the Challenge,” the campaign positions the vehicle as more than a muscle car — it represents the mindset of embracing competition, pushing limits, and rising to every opportunity. Developed and produced in Florida.

The Approach

AAEC co-directed and creative-directed the campaign from concept through production — creative direction, art direction, cinematography, aerial coverage, location scouting, music selection, and photography — delivering a polished commercial aligned with the Dodge brand.
