The Brief

Kwame & Kruma needed campaign imagery and a short commercial that put their headwear in a setting as considered as the product itself — not a studio backdrop, but a real room with real weight to it.

The Approach

We shot a full editorial set alongside the commercial spot, framing every portrait around composition and light rather than product-shot convention, then cut a companion film for the same campaign.
