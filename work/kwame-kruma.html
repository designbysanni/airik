Kwame N Kruma — Knight Armor

The Brief

The first production took place in the California desert, where the natural landscape and earth-tone palette complemented the brand's handcrafted apparel and accessories. The second shifted to a refined interior setting, using architecture, music, and luxury aesthetics to reinforce the sophistication behind the collection.

The Approach

AAEC led both campaigns from concept through post-production — art direction, cinematography, drone operation, location selection, editing, and color finishing — working closely with the designer to translate his vision into a cinematic brand experience with a lean production crew.
