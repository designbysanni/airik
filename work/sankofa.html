Sankofa Village Wellness Center — Grand Opening

The Brief

Led by Wright to Wellness Collective in partnership with Rush University Medical Center, Erie Family Health Centers, the YMCA, and local community partners, the grand opening marked the culmination of more than three years of planning — attended by civic leaders, healthcare professionals, and community stakeholders including Chicago Mayor Brandon Johnson.

The Approach

AAEC led the event's visual storytelling from production through post-production — capturing the ceremony, conducting stakeholder interviews, directing on-site content, and producing a recap film that documents a landmark investment in community health.
