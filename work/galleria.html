Galleria From The Living Room

An original art exhibition series created under the Live From The Living Room brand — merging contemporary art with hip-hop culture.

Galleria — Event Film

The Brief

Designed to expand the LFTLR platform beyond music into the broader creative community, the exhibition brought together more than 600 attendees and showcased the work of 40+ artists across multiple events — an immersive space where artists, collectors, musicians, and creatives connect and collaborate. The series has been covered by Chicago press for reviving contemporary Chicago art.

The Approach

AAEC conceived, produced, and executed every aspect of the experience — branding, marketing, art direction, exhibition design, event production, media coverage, and installation, including constructing custom gallery walls. A series of short artist-spotlight films introduced audiences to participating creatives and drove attendance across social platforms.
