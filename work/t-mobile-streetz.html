Metro By T-Mobile: Voice of The Streetz

A community activation designed to strengthen Metro by T-Mobile's connection with Chicago's music and youth culture — a live in-store performance featuring artist Lil Moo, in partnership with The Streetz radio.

Voice of The Streetz — Metro By T-Mobile

The Brief

Hosted in partnership with The Streetz radio, the event transformed a Metro retail location into a space where music, entertainment, and community engagement came together — an authentic brand experience for local audiences.

The Approach

AAEC documented the event through video and photography while producing social content that captured the energy of the performance — visuals that feel culturally authentic while showcasing the brand's commitment to the communities it serves.
