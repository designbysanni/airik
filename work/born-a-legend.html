Born A Legend: 33rd — The Documentary

A feature-length documentary chronicling the life and legacy of Chicago artist Freebandz Trey Trey — from the Jeffrey Manor neighborhood to the national music scene.

Born A Legend — Documentary Promo

The Brief

Once a close collaborator of Future and Freeband Gang, Trey Trey built a national career that included touring and working alongside artists such as Tory Lanez. Originally envisioned as a companion piece to his album 33rd, the project evolved into a tribute documentary following his tragic passing — preserving his story and impact for his family, fans, and the Chicago music community.

The Approach

AAEC directed, produced, co-edited, and conducted interviews for the 80-minute documentary from concept through completion — coordinating interviews, shaping the narrative, and overseeing post-production. More than a music documentary, it explores legacy, loss, resilience, and the realities facing young artists.
